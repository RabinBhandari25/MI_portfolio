    • People’s Power

    For my give back project, I chose a project proposal, titled "People’s Power," which presents an agrivoltaic farm in Kamloops, developed as part of the ENST 3110 course. It was presented in class and submitted to Fortis BC for further analysis. The primary goal of the project is to empower local farmers and the community by providing a means of generating both regenerative energy and food simultaneously. This approach aims to enhance food and energy security while reducing reliance on non-renewable sources.
