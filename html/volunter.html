    • Fostering sustainability

    Serving as a Sustainability Ambassador at TRU was a transformative experience that deepened my commitment to environmental sustainability. Through peer outreach, I developed strong communication and leadership skills while educating students on sustainability issues and promoting eco-friendly initiatives like the Fill it Forward campaign and the BioBlitz event. These experiences reinforced the importance of small, consistent actions in creating positive environmental change. Additionally, engaging with a diverse community helped me develop a deeper understanding of different cultural perspectives on sustainability, strengthening my ability to lead collaboratively and inclusively for long-term impact.
